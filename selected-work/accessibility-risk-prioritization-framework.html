Accessibility Risk & Prioritization Framework

Prioritization Accessibility Strategy

Challenge

Accessibility opportunities existed across numerous products, workflows, and touchpoints. Resources were limited, making it impossible to address every issue at the same time.

Leadership needed a practical way to understand where accessibility investment would have the greatest impact.

Approach

I developed a risk-based prioritization framework that evaluated accessibility opportunities through multiple lenses, including user impact, audience reach, severity, legal risk, and business considerations.

The framework helped leaders make informed decisions about where to focus support, remediation efforts, and accessibility resources.

What Was Created

  • Accessibility risk assessment framework
  • Prioritization methodology
  • Executive recommendations
  • Stakeholder communication materials
  • Support planning model
  • Accessibility investment roadmap

Outcomes

  • Improved visibility into accessibility risk across experiences
  • Helped leadership prioritize limited resources
  • Supported more strategic planning conversations
  • Created a repeatable decision-making process
  • Aligned accessibility efforts with business priorities
