Risk-and-impact result: Critical is reserved for an essential, no-alternate-path journey with documented risk or an active intervention; High signals several elevated factors or a timely delivery window; Managed means visible risk with a bounded support route; Monitor means baseline coverage is appropriate. These are human assessment categories in this historical reconstruction.

Judgment remains explicit: the record retains the risk-and-impact result, then allows a separately documented portfolio-lead adjustment when new context changes the call. A leadership request to fund a touchpoint is captured separately in the support lane and funding rationale; it does not rewrite the risk-and-impact result.

Portfolio taxonomy: Initiative → Domain → Workstream → Project. A workstream is an organizational grouping, not a piece of remediation. The accessibility audit / remediation is an active intervention linked to Careers website: External; it does not create a duplicate touchpoint record.
