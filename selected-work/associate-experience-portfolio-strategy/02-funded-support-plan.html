Funded does not mean “fully fixed”: it means deeper support capacity is planned for that touchpoint. Assessment cue: the support cue summarizes touchpoint signals and current context; scope and checkpoint are confirmed by the portfolio lead.

Separate the concepts: support lane is a touchpoint field with the choices Baseline or Funded. An active intervention is a linked record only when there is current work to track, such as the Careers website accessibility audit / remediation. Workstream remains an organizational taxonomy field.
