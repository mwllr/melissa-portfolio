# Airtable-Style Wireframe Views

Sixteen linked, low-fidelity views for the Touchpoints case-study mockup. They use representative, public-safe data and make provenance visible: historical reconstruction, proposed activation, and a possible future AI extension are separate concepts.

Start with `01-all-touchpoints.html`.

Views 01–04, 06, 13, and 16 model the historic inventory, prioritization method, roadmap-timing guardrail, minimum-information intake, and service patterns. View 09 is a public-safe portfolio-audience reframing of a real leadership strategy readout, not a replica of the original artifact. Views 05, 07, 08, 14, and 15 are proposed activation examples; they must not be presented as historically delivered functionality. Any future AI-assisted triage appears only in the clearly marked field-glossary extension and is not historical evidence.

`10-field-glossary.html` documents the working field model, including format, selectable options, provenance, and decision purpose. Its final rows are explicitly future-extension fields rather than historical capabilities.

`11-careers-website-record.html`, `12-required-workflow-record.html`, and `13-new-touchpoint-intake.html` are historical reconstruction examples: two records plus the minimum-information path for adding a row. `14-draft-touchpoint-record.html` and `15-safety-check-in-app-record.html` are proposed activation examples.
